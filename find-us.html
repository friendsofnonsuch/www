How To Find Us

Please click here to access map information relating to our site

For those of you with SatNav the post code is SM3 8AL

Please enter by the Cheam Gate Entrance on the Ewell Road (A232) .  At the sharp bend in the road turn into the park entrance.  Follow the road down and round to the right.  Nonsuch mansion will be in front of you.  There is a car park off to the right and the entrance to the service wing museum and nonsuch palace gallery is on your left by the entrance to the formal gardens.

Public toilets and a cafe are situated at the front of the mansion house.

Google Maps

Left, you can see a map of Nonsuch park itself. The marker represents where in the park the mansion house is situated. 

You can reach us by train or bus, for the train you need to take a train to Cheam station. If you are travelling by bus, then please download the bus station map found here

Car parking is available on site for free, however please check closing times on the day.
