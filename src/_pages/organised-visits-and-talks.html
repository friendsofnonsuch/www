Organised Visits and Talks

The Service Wing Museum will be opened for pre-arranged guided tours from March to November

Groups may view the -
Dairy, Kitchen, Sculleries, Larders & Laundries
and the Nonsuch collection of Stained Glass

Refreshments are available locally in the Nonsuch park Cafe

We welcome groups of any age and would be happy to discuss your specific requirements

Our organisation is also willing to provide a speaker on various topics relating to Nonsuch
