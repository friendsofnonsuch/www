Nonsuch Palace Gallery   ►

Well worth a visit, the Nonsuch Palace model is spectacular and took two years to make. When visiting you will be able to see the model as well as read up on the history and learn about the archaeological dig which took place to discover more information about the palace. Learn about how the model was made on this page.
