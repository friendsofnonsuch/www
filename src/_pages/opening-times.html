Opening Times

Nonsuch Palace Gallery (Open year round)

Is open every Sunday throughout the year from 12pm - 4pm. Entry is FREE. Visit to see the model and history of Henry VIII's Nonsuch Palace.

The Service Wing Museum (2022 Season)

The Service Wing Museum will be open on the Second and Fourth Sundays in the month and Bank Holidays. It will be open from 12pm - 4pm (Last entry 3.30pm.). It will NOT, however, be open on the extra Bank Holiday for the King's Coronation, Monday 8th May 2023.

Entry is FREE. Visit to see the Georgian/Victorian dairy, sculleries, kitchen, pantries, game larder and laundries.

Vehicular access via Cheam Gate, with parking near to the Mansion House.
