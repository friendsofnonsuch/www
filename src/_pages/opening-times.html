Opening Times

Nonsuch Palace Gallery (Open year round)

Is open every Sunday throughout the year from 12pm - 4pm. Entry is FREE. Visit to see the model and history of Henry VIII's Nonsuch Palace.

The Service Wing Museum (2021 Season)

For August and September 2021, the Service Wing Museum will be open on the Second and Fourth Sundays in the month, and the August Bank Holiday Monday, from 12pm - 4pm (Last entry 3:30pm). Entry is FREE. Visit to see the Georgian/Victorian dairy, sculleries, kitchen, pantries, game larder and laundries.

Vehicular access via Cheam Gate, with parking near to the Mansion House.
