The Farmer Family   ►

Learn about the history of the farmer family at Nonsuch on the following page. To help you explore the history we've added family tree diagrams on the page.

The first Farmer to purchase Nonsuch was Samuel Farmer of Shoreditch in 1799, from there we can trace back his family history for at least 8 generations.

If you have any information relating to the family history especially the Irish branch of the family and how they link into the Farmer family at Shoreditch The Friends would be delighted to hear from you
