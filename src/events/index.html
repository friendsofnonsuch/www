Opening times of the Nonsuch Palace Model and Service Wing Museum

NONSUCH PALACE MODEL - FREE entry
Is open every Sunday throughout the year from 2pm - 5pm from April to September, and 11am - 3pm from October to March.

THE SERVICE WING MUSEUM - FREE entry
Is only open during the summer from Easter weekend until the end of September on the Second and Fourth Sundays of the month, and Bank Holidays from 2pm - 5pm, although the Museum will not be open on the changed May Day Bank Holiday this year, which will occur on a Friday.
