Summer Opening Times
from April until the end of September 2019

Nonsuch Palace Gallery (Open throughout Winter)
Is open every Sunday throughout the year from 2pm - 5pm from April to September, and 11am - 3pm from October to March. Entry is FREE. Visit to see the model and history of Henry VIII's Nonsuch Palace.

The Service Wing Museum (Closing for Winter after 22nd September 2019)
Will be open only during the summer from April until the end of September on the Second and Fourth Sundays in the month, and all Bank Holidays from 2pm - 5pm (Last entry 4:30pm). Entry is FREE. Visit to see the Georgian/Victorian dairy, sculleries, kitchen, pantries, game larder and laundries.

Vehicular access via Cheam Gate, with parking near to the Mansion House.
